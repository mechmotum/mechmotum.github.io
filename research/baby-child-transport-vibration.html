Vibrations of Babies and Children During Human-powered Transport

Summary

This project's goal is to characterize the vibrations experienced by babies and children during transportation in strollers and cargo bicycles with a eye towards consequences and improvements to health and comfort.

Theme Bicycle Engineering Current Researchers
Last Worked On July 2025 Past Researchers Gabriele Dell'Orto, Jesper Meijerink, Thomas Valk, Santo Bruijn, Alexander Meyers, Dirk-Jan van Ommeren, Jelmer Riegstra, Renate van Rooijen
